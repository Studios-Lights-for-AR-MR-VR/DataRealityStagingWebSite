Gustavo Gomez

Founder & President

Gustavo A Gomez founded Data Reality Softwares with the goals of innovating and transforming the technology world. As the current President his mission is to provide leadership with the focus to deliver technology to customers on a global scale. With over 20 years of experience in Information Technology Mr. Gomez passion for technology has blossom from tinkering with his parents VHS player to creating content that is used by millions.

In his younger days Mr. Gomez was involved and contributed to open source projects such as GAIM (currently called pidgin), JavaMail-Cryto API, and other GNU projects. With a passion to bridging the gap in education for technology, he taught basic computer skills to elderly and the handicapped at his local community technology center. Created a new web application for the Human Resources department at his mother's company so that employees can have an online contact list that was easily searchable and provided profiles to give a face to a name/title. Was a instructor of computer programming at Vassar College teaching object oriented programming, game design, and operating system principles. Consulted businesses ranging in various sizes from 2 to 9000. And for the past ten years Mr. Gomez has become a Subject Matter Expert in IT administration, Electronic Content Management, Software as a Service, and Cloud technologies.

In his spare time Gustavo loves to play golf, visit the beach, enjoy nature, and spend time with family and friends. He goes to church weekly, and volunteers with organizations with a focus in S.T.E.A.M.

Mr. Gomez graduated from the State University of New York, Binghamton University, in 2005 with a degree in Bachelor of Science in Computer Science
